Services Mulch Installation

Our Mulch Installation service is a great way to improve the appearance of your yard while also protecting your plants and soil from erosion. We can install mulch in any color you choose, so you can create a look that's perfect for your home.

Reviews Carousel

Carousel

There are many benefits to having a Mulch Installation service provided by ProPhone, a Lawn Care company. First, mulching helps to retain soil moisture, which is important for healthy plants and grass. Mulching also blocks out the sun so that weeds don’t grow as easily. Additionally, mulching can help to add organic matter to the soil, which is beneficial for plant growth.

ProPhone’s Mulch Installation service includes the installation of a layer of mulch around your plants and trees. We use quality mulch materials that will help improve the health of your landscape. Our team will also take care in installing the mulch around your plants so that there is no damage done.

If you would like to improve the health and appearance of your landscape, then you should consider booking a Mulch Installation service with ProPhone. Contact us today for more information or to book an appointment!
